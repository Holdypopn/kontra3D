Gathering System
================

Inputs:
-------
PlayerStats
	- Health
	- Fortitude
Equipment
	- Weapon
	- Misc (Map, Flashlight, ...)


Processing:
-----------
- Pool of items with own rarity/probability
- "Baselevel" were you just get the flat probabilities of the items and just "roll the dice"
- Stats and Equipment improve the probability

- A "Focus" value - What am I looking for?
- You always look for one "TimeUnit" and get a result -> now you can decide to go on with the search or come back


Outputs:
--------
Items!
